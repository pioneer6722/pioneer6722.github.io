Pioneer Robotics

Who Are We?

Hello! We are team Pioneer, also know as team 6722. We are a school based robotics program that strives to go to states and beyond. We have 6 teams as of the 2024-2025 season and this year we had 3 teams qualify for states. We would like to grow that number and that is why we have put together this website.
